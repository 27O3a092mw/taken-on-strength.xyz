Private Arthur Alway, 127580

Date of Birth: October 6, 1895 in either Alveston or Thornbury England1 map of the area.

Family: On the 1901 and 1915 England and Wales census I was able to find that the family consisted of father, George Alway; mother Annie; Arthur himself, and younger brother William.

Occupation: on the 1911 England and Wales census Arthur was 15, and listed as a "milk boy on farm". When he moved to Canada in 1913 he listed his occupation as "farm labour" and he did the same in 1915 when he enlisted in the CEF

Moved to Canada: Arthur arrived in Canada at Quebec City, on June 24, 1913 on the Royal George. When he arrived he listed his final destination as Ingersoll, Ontario
